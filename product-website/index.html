Butterfly Comb

On this page, we'll be discussing butterfly combs. We chose this product for its good design and to help those unfamiliar with this type of tool learn how to use it.

This tool is based on a butterfly knife, but replaces the blade with a comb, allowing users to learn to handle it safely and without risk. The parts it includes are:


  • The comb


  • Safety handle


  • Bite handle


  • Swivel pins


  • Locking mechanism


  • Tang


  • Pins


    • These are hair combs styled to look and function exactly like a balisong (butterfly knife). Instead of a sharp metal blade, the pivoting mechanism flips open to reveal a standard hair comb. They are commonly used as safe, legal practice tools for learning flipping tricks, or simply as a novelty hair styling accessory.
